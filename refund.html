Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. General Policy

SyncData Guard offers a 7-day free trial before purchase. We strongly encourage all users to fully evaluate the product using the trial before making a purchase decision.

2. No-Refund Policy

Once a license key has been issued, refunds are not available as a general rule. Due to the nature of digital software licenses, issued keys cannot be revoked or recovered.

3. Exceptional Cases

Refunds may be considered on a case-by-case basis in the following situations:

  • The product fails to provide its core described functionality
  • A technical issue prevents use entirely and cannot be resolved after support assistance
  • A duplicate payment has occurred

4. How to Request a Refund

For refund inquiries, please contact support@syncdataguard.com with your order number and the reason for your request.

5. Trial Recommendation

Please test the product thoroughly using the 7-day free trial before purchasing. Refund requests submitted after extensive trial usage will generally not be accepted.
